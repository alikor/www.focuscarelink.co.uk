Live-in Care Assistant

Position:     Live-in Care Assistant 

Report to:   Manager & Care Co-ordinator

Wage:         Competitive Rates

Location:    Central London

The successful candidate will be required to live in the service user's home and will be responsible for the personal care and household support of the client.  You will have your own room, and be required to remain in the home for 22 out of 24 hours per day. You will be required to assist with daily activities, personal care, prompting medication and meal preparation.

The ideal applicant will hold a minimum qualification QCF 2 in Health and Social Care, and experience of working with older people or people with disabilities.

If you do not have the required qualification, but think that you have the potential and passion for the task; if you are a compassionate and reliable person we will be happy to support you with training.

We are looking for an enthusiastic, energetic, outgoing person who can work individually or within a team.

To apply please email us your CV to recruitment@focuscarelink.co.uk
