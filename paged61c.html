Live-in Care

Live-in Care is a great alternative to a care home.  It allows the client to stay in the comfort and privacy of their own home while enjoying an unrivalled level of 1-2-1 support, 24 hours a day. What’s more, many people are surprised to realise that live-in care is an affordable alternative to care homes.

Combining personal care, housekeeping, and any other elements of support needed with the benefit of companionship, our live-in care service is tailored to the specific needs of the individual, while allowing them to live independently, retain their identity and maintain their mental well being and peace of mind by staying in their own home.  

If the client requires a specialist level of care or a care worker who can drive, is happy to be around pets, or shares a particular hobby or interest, we can work to find the right person for the role.  There are no worries about feeling vulnerable or alone at night, as the care worker will be there should the client need them.  

The appointed care worker will spend time with the client and their loved ones or family to design a bespoke care plan based on the client’s requirements. Focus Care Link will then provide all the support needed both for the service user and the care worker: reviewing the service regularly to ensure that it meets the needs of the client, offering cover should the care worker need any time off, and ensuring that the care worker is fulfilling their role to the client’s satisfaction.
