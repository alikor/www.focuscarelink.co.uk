Location:      The Following London Post Codes:

Camden:     NW1, NW2, NW3, NW5, NW6, N1, N4, N5, N6, N7, N10, N19
Waltham Forest:          E10 E11, E15, E17
Barking & Dagenham: RM6, RM8. RM9, RM10, IG11

We are looking for Health Care Worker candidates who have a good command of the English language.

Knowing any of the following languages is an advantage but not necessary:  

  • Cantonese
  • Greek
  • Mandarin
  • Bengali
  • Gujrati
  • Portuguese

Work Hours available:
