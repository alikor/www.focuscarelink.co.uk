Domestic & Shopping Services

We work with a number of clients who find it hard to maintain their usual standard of housekeeping, helping with these tasks to make life less difficult. Whether housebound, living with reduced mobility, recently out of hospital or finding domestic tasks difficult for any other reason, our Domestic & Shopping Services can reduce the burden.

Our domestic cleaning team will do as much or as little as each client requires. By fitting their services around specific needs and requirements, we provide the combination of assistance and independence that every one of our clients need.

In addition to our cleaning services, we also offer a shopping service to reduce the number of chores that our clients have to do. Even those who are still able and independent in other ways can find the regular shop a struggle: we remove this burden. Working to the shopping lists that our clients provide, we visit the shops on their behalf and deliver at a time that is convenient to them. We will also unpack and put away the shopping, giving them peace of mind.
